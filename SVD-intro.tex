For every $m \times n$ matrix $A$, there exist orthonormal $\{u_1,\ldots,u_m\}$ and $\{v_1,\ldots,v_n\}$, together with nonnegative $s_1 \ge \cdots \ge s_n$ with
\begin{align*}
Av_1 &= s_1 u_1\\
Av_2 &= s_2 u_2\\
&\vdots\\
Av_n &= s_n u_n.
\end{align*}
